HEALTHY PLANEAT

is a sustainable food smartphone application and online platform designed to help you achieve an affordable, healthy, and environmentally sustainable diet

Did you know?

The food and agriculture industry emits 30% of total global greenhouse gas emissions, uses 70% of freshwater resources, and is responsible for 60% of deforestation. Your diet choice can reduce these environmental impacts.

Diet and climate change

Choosing a diet low in meat can reduce your carbon footprint

Diet CO2 equivalent per day
High meat 7.19 kilos
Medium meat 5.63 kilos
Low meat 4.67 kilos
Pescatarian 3.91 kilos
Vegetarian 3.81 kilos
Vegan 2.89 kilos

Co-benefits of a sustainable diet

Mediterranean, pescatarian, and vegetarian diets reduce health risks including heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and some cancers. Plant based diets have the potential to cut GHG emissions by up to 70%, save up to 8 million lives by 2050, and Up to 1.5 trillion dollars from avoided climate-related damages.
